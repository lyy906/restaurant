Our Story

Hidden History. more than you expect.

Corner 17 has opened for less than two years. However, its owner has operated Joy Luck Buffet, another popular Chinese restaurant in St. Louis, for more than 5 years. His experience has successfully transformed Corner 17 to a Chinese restaurant that well understands its customers' needs


Authenticity. See for yourself.

The goal of Corner 17 is to bring the most authentic Chinese flavor and experience to its customers. As such, it hires professional chiefs who specialize in making hand-made noodles and you can even see how they make the noodles from your seats.


And lastly, this one. Disserts.

The disserts, especially milk teas, have been the important feature of Corner 17. To bring the best quality and anthenticity of the products, we imported special machines to produce the best milk teas you can find around the campus.
